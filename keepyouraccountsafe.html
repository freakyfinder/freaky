#9 - Monday - 18-09-2023

I would like to remind my fellow Robloxians to keep their accounts safe and secure by any means necessary. Remember to use strong passwords that are hard to guess, and don't use the same password for everything. Since its not 2012, I don't need to remind you to keep your password to yourself. But even then- if you have anything good in your account, they will come for it, and your password alone isn't going to save you. It only takes three minutes to set up the 2-factor Authentication. (I would recommend choosing the option for the Google Authenticator App, as it provides more security and ease than receiving codes through email) It might be a minor inconvenience to put in that code with every log-in, but it's even more inconvenient losing all your limiteds to cybercriminals. And remember- do not fall for password scams, cookie/inspect element scams, or any type of trick asking you to upload your avatar to some site for a baloney art project. If you think your account may be at risk, remember to sign out from all other sessions to refresh your cookie, change your password, and verify that your email is still yours.
