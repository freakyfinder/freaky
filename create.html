Properties











Anchored: Makes the part stuck to one place. Can't be moved ingame.

CanCollide: Gives the part a hitbox.

Locked: Makes the part selectable in studio. (You can still select the part via Explorer)

Transparency: Makes the part visible/invisible. Transparency at 0 makes it 100% visible, and 1 makes it invisible.

Make parts of the client visible, like explorer, properties etc.
