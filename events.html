Grand Melee Tournament
Status: Active

Fight through three days of Grand Melee matches, starting Friday 26th to Sunday 28th.

Round 1(Friday) - The top 10 players with the highest K/D ratio will move on to Round 2
Round 2(Saturday) - The top 5 players with the highest K/D ratio will move on to Round 3
Round 3 -Grand Finale (Sunday) - The top 3 players with the highest K/D ratio will win!

Note: All you have to do to enter the tournament is play round 1 and get more than 5 KO's

All winners will receive an exclusive prize hat.
3rd) 250 Ro-Bits.
2nd) 500 Ro-Bits.
1st) 1000 Ro-Bits

Please note: This event will take place in Novetus! If you need help installing Novetus, we'll help you out step by step how to do so. Don't let that get in the way from you entering the tournament!
