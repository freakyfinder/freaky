Level Calculator Info

This calculator is based on Miked's and Clockwork's level calculator formula, but it has been updated with current stats to match the original level calculator. It also shows how likely you would have been chosen for BC back in 2007 and overall gives you a number indicating how worthy of a Robloxian you are. Enter your stats to see your level!

If you get NaN for your result, it means one of your numbers wasn't typed like a number.
