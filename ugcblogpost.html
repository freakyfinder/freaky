#5 - Thursday 14-09-2023

Roblox just killed UGC. And you might be wondering what I'm talking about. Well, Roblox is going to change UGC massively, and this new update is making so many UGC creators quit. But what is this update excactly, and what what will this do to Roblox in generel. Let's discuss that.

As you might already know, RDC was recently held, and RDC means new features. One of those features, does change UGC really much in a bad way. You might already know about public UGC coming out soon, and if you know Roblox, you know the only thing they care about is money.

The new update I'm talking about here, is Roblox making UGC even more expensive for creators, and making it almost impossible to actually buy and own UGC. You might also know about UGC limiteds. And basically what this new update is, is Roblox making EVERY UGC item on the catalog a limited. And you're probably thinking that this isn't too bad, but you're wrong. So you see, to make a Roblox UGC limited item, you must buy copies/restock on copies of the item for the users to buy. This usually costs a bit of Robux for every copy. Now what Roblox is going to do, is forcing UGC creators to buy copies, for copy they make. Making it even harder to make money off of UGC items.

One of the questions you are probably wondering, is "How much is it going to cost per copy?"

Hats: 20 Robux per copy

Face accessories: 10 Robux per copy

Back accessories: 30 Robux per copy

At first, you are probably thinking that this ain't too bad. But considering that the average UGC creator makes a couple hundreds, and some creators make thousands of different items, and they each have around a couple thousands, and some even in the hundreds of thousands and millions of copies. If we for an example took a hat, and said it had 25,000 copies, that would cost the creator 500,000 Robux just to make those 25,000 copies. And considering that 25,000 copies ain't even that high, it's just crazy to think about.

I really hope Roblox would reconsider this, even though I strongly doubt it.
