Ode to the Anachronists

#19 - 04-02-2024 by insaneshane

After a long duration of planning, coordinating, building, and other agonizing processes, the First Grand Melee finally comes to an end.
This was our first successful event, and our first event held in Novetus.
Even though it didn't go perfectly, it was still a blast seeing people blow each other up for three hours over two days. (Using deprecated systems on a hardly-stable engine with people connecting from all around the world to a single proxy should obviously result in some errors.)
I am also very happy to have had my hat, the Anachronist Apotheosis, used as the prize.
I would like to once again give my personal congratulations to the three winners, and a thank you to everyone who played, and those who intended to play but couldn't.
And to those who couldn't- do not feel too bad about missing this event, there is still more to come, and more to earn!

The three winners has received the Anachronist Apotheosis.
