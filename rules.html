Rules

I will begin with the most important rule. You should use common sense. There will always be unwritten rules, but if you use your head and common sense, it wouldn't be a problem. These rules will also apply to the Roblox group, and Discord server. We will not tolerate any racism, sexism or anything like that. Treat others with respect, as you would do in real life. Of course we don't allow any porn, or anything like that, but mature topics in forms of swearing are allowed. On this site, you can advertise what you want, as long as it stays Roblox related. You can advertise your game, group, work, website as long as it is Roblox related. For example, if you just release your brand new steam game, you are not allowed to advertise it, but you are allowed to advertise your Roblox game. Also, let's try to keep spamming and other bug abuse related stuff from happening. Thank you for keeping the community safe.
